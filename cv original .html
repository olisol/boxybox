Mnemonic — Elfeherest: UX Research & Redesign
UX research and design project for Elfeherest, an archive platform used by human rights researchers for OSINT investigations. Conducted heuristic analysis and remote usability testing with both new and advanced users across moderator and analyst workflows. Developed A/B prototypes for the task management feature, validated findings through a second round of user interviews, and delivered a redesign proposal with actionable recommendations.
Roles: UX research, heuristic analysis, usability testing, A/B prototyping, UI design
